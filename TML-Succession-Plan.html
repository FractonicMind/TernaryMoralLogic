A Legacy of Accountability

The succession plan for Ternary Moral Logic (TML), a framework for embedding permanent ethical constraints into AI.

What is Ternary Moral Logic?

TML is an open-source system of prior restraint and immutable auditing for automated actions, ensuring AI operates within non-negotiable ethical boundaries.

The 180-Day Succession Plan

The transition is structured in four distinct phases to ensure a smooth and responsible handoff of stewardship.

Phase 1: Notification

Days 1-30

Public announcement and formal notification to all recommended custodian organizations to begin the process.

Phase 2: Response

Days 31-90

Potential custodians evaluate the proposal, assess their capabilities, and confirm their commitment to the TML mission.

Phase 3: Preparation

Days 91-150

Technical and knowledge transfer begins, including moving blockchain infrastructure access and smart contract custody.

Phase 4: Handoff

Days 151-180

Final transfer of all assets and responsibilities. The new Stewardship Council becomes fully operational.

The Mission Continues
