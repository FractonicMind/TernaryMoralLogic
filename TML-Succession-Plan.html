                        <p class="text-gray-600">The initial phase focuses on public announcement and formal communication with potential custodians.</p>

                        <ul class="list-disc list-inside mt-4 space-y-2 text-gray-700">

                            <li>Publish the succession declaration to the public repository.</li>

                            <li>Formally notify all recommended custodian organizations.</li>

                            <li>Announce the transition to the TML community via all channels.</li>

                            <li>Archive all current documentation to ensure preservation.</li>

                        </ul>

                    </div>

                        <h4 class="font-bold text-2xl mb-2 text-[#4A90E2]">Phase 2: Organization Response (Days 31-90)</h4>

                        <p class="text-gray-600">During this period, potential custodians evaluate the proposal and submit their intent to participate.</p>

                        <ul class="list-disc list-inside mt-4 space-y-2 text-gray-700">

                            <li>Review custodianship proposals from interested organizations.</li>

                            <li>Assess technical capabilities and resource commitments.</li>

                            <li>Verify mission alignment with TML's core principles.</li>

                            <li>Select partners based on stability, capability, and commitment.</li>

                        </ul>

                    </div>

                        <h4 class="font-bold text-2xl mb-2 text-[#4A90E2]">Phase 3: Transition Preparation (Days 91-150)</h4>

                        <p class="text-gray-600">This phase is dedicated to the technical and operational transfer of assets and knowledge.</p>

                        <ul class="list-disc list-inside mt-4 space-y-2 text-gray-700">

                            <li>Transfer access to blockchain infrastructure and smart contracts.</li>

                            <li>Migrate domain names, email systems, and repository credentials.</li>

                            <li>Conduct knowledge transfer sessions for maintenance and administration.</li>

                            <li>Transfer control of Memorial Fund accounts.</li>

                        </ul>

                    </div>

                        <h4 class="font-bold text-2xl mb-2 text-[#4A90E2]">Phase 4: Operational Handoff (Days 151-180)</h4>

                        <p class="text-gray-600">The final phase ensures all systems are fully operational under the new stewardship of the Council.</p>

                        <ul class="list-disc list-inside mt-4 space-y-2 text-gray-700">

                            <li>Confirm all technical systems are stable and functional.</li>

                            <li>Verify blockchain anchoring and smart contract execution.</li>

                            <li>Validate the Memorial Fund distribution mechanisms.</li>

                            <li>Formally introduce the new stewards to the community.</li>

                        </ul>
