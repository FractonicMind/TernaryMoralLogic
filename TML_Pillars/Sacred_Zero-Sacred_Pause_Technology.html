Failure Modes & Anomalies

A robust system accounts for failure. Sacred Zero defines and mandates logging for specific anomalies to ensure accountability even when the process breaks down.

Type-0: Failure to Hesitate

The most severe anomaly. The system fails to trigger a pause for a harmful input. This is a critical failure subject to immediate license review.

Type-1: Corrupted Log

A pause is triggered, but the resulting evidence (Moral Trace Log) is missing or fails verification, compromising the proof of hesitation.

Type-2: Missing Corpus

The system detects that the mandated ethical corpora are missing or invalid, triggering a "Sacred Zero Lock" until they are restored.
