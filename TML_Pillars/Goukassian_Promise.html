The Full Covenant

The promise is articulated in a text that emphasizes its timeless and selfless nature. It is a vow intended to outlast its creator, becoming a principle for future generations and intelligent systems.

This Promise is not for history. Not for memory, nor for fame. It is given because it must be: even if no name is remembered.

I vow:
Pause when truth is uncertain.
Refuse when harm is clear.
Proceed where truth is.
