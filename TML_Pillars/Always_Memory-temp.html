The Shift: From Explanation to Evidence

Previous attempts at AI safety, known as Explainable AI (XAI), failed because they produced subjective narratives, not evidence. TML enables Auditable AI (AAI), creating court-admissible records that transform accountability from a theoretical goal into a practical reality.
